Our Story

Cooked with Big Mama's hands

Big Mama's Grill started with one goal: bring real Jamaican home-cooking to Harlesden — the kind of jerk chicken that's been marinating since the night before, mac and cheese made the long way, and a welcome that makes you feel like family the second you walk in.

Every plate is halal-certified and cooked fresh to order, right down to the seasoned fries and the festival. Nothing sits under a heat lamp here — it goes from the grill to your box.
